📘 Tournament Format

  • Each team will play league matches within their division.
  • Top 4 teams from each division qualify for the semifinals of their division.
  • 🥇 The two finalists of Division 2 will be promoted to Division 1 for the next tournament.
  • 🥇 The two finalists of Division 3 will be promoted to Division 2.
  • 🔻 Bottom 2 teams of Division 1 will be relegated to Division 2.
  • 🔻 Bottom 2 teams of Division 2 will be relegated to Division 3.

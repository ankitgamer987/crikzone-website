📜 CRIKZONE MATCH RULES 🏏️

🎮 IN-GAME RULES

  • 🚫 No Shuffling at Any Time: No movement leg/off side or front/back allowed.
  • 🤖 AI Fielding Required: Set "AI Selects Human Field" to Yes.
  • ⚠️ Bowling Penalty: Each Early Jump or Weak Release = 2 wides.
  • 🎮 All Games must be played on Hardest Difficulty.

⚠️ GLITCH & DISCONNECT RULES

  • ✅ Glitches: Must be accepted. No replays.
  • 💾 Game Hang: Save & resume. Restore any boundary or wicket missed when resuming.
  • 🧑‍⚖️ DRS-Hang Logic:
    • If Umpire gave OUT before crash = it is OUT
    • If Umpire gave NOT OUT before crash = it is NOT OUT

📌 TEAM RULES

  • 🚫 No Player Edits: No changes to name, OVR, or attributes. Violators forfeit the match. Applies to friendlies too.
  • 🚫 No running after hitting the stumps.
  • ⚠️ No Manual Field Changes: Do not manually move keeper or fielders. Just enable "AI Selects Human Field".

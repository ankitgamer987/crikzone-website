📝 In-Match Guidelines

  • ⚙️ AI Field: Must be ON – Go to settings and set "AI selects Human fields" = ✅ True.
  • 🚷 No Shuffling: Do not move forward, back, left or right before delivery.
  • ❌ No Early Jump / Weak Release: Not allowed under any circumstance.
  • 🔁 Bowling Balance: In limited overs, min. 30% of overs must be spin, and min. 30% must be fast/medium-fast. No rule in tests.
  • 🚫 No Defence + Run: Blocking and running is not allowed.
  • 🧪 Accept Glitches: All in-game glitches are to be accepted. No replays or restarts.
  • 🧑‍⚖️ Hung Umpire Decisions: If DRS can't be taken due to game hang, umpire’s decision is final.
  • 📋 Official Formats: Only T20, ODI, and Test formats are used for tournaments. Others are for friendlies only.
  • 🕒 Minimum Overs Rule: Once a match starts, minimum 20 overs must be played unless there’s an extreme reason. Repeat offenders = admin penalty.
  • 🎭 No Player Edits: Editing player OVR, skills, or attributes is strictly not allowed.

🔒 Community Guidelines

  • 🛑 No toxicity, hate speech, racism, or abuse of any kind.
  • ✅ Respect all members. Healthy banter is fine — personal attacks are not.
  • 🎮 Use `#match-schedule` to plan games and `#scorecards` to post results.
  • 📜 Follow match rules listed in the Match Rules page strictly.
  • 🚫 No cheating, exploiting, or disconnecting mid-match.
  • 🔇 Do not spam messages, links, or self-promotions outside `#promotion`.
  • ⚖️ Mods have the final say. Don't argue with decisions in public channels.
  • 🕊 Keep it clean, respectful, and fun. Let the bat and ball do the talking! 🏏

🔒 Community Guidelines

  • 🤝 Treat all members with respect and kindness. No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
  • 🧼 Avoid excessive swearing and offensive language. Keep conversations friendly.
  • 🚫 No spamming or irrelevant messages. Stay on-topic and keep the chat clean.
  • 📣 No self-promotion or advertising without mod approval — this includes other servers or personal projects.
  • 📜 Follow Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines at all times.
  • 🔞 No NSFW or inappropriate content of any kind.
  • 📂 Use the correct channels for their intended purposes — check descriptions when in doubt.
  • 🎮 No cheating, hacking, or exploiting in league matches. Fair play only.
  • 🔐 Respect others’ privacy — no sharing personal information or private convos.
  • 🆘 Report issues or rule-breaking to moderators instead of handling it yourself.
  • 💬 Engage positively in discussions and be active in events to build community spirit.
  • 🎧 In voice channels, avoid loud/disruptive sounds and be respectful to all speakers.
  • ⚖️ Follow mod/admin instructions — they help ensure a fun, fair environment.
